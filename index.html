Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (“App”) collects, uses, and protects your data when you connect your WHOOP account through OAuth.

1. Data We Access

Depending on permissions granted, the App may access WHOOP data such as:

  • Profile information (for example: first name, last name, email)
  • Recovery, sleep, workouts, cycles, and body measurements

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide analytics, dashboards, and personalized insights
  • To display your WHOOP metrics inside the App
  • To improve reliability and user experience

3. Legal Basis and Consent

Data is accessed only after your explicit OAuth authorization. You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting the integration.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is transmitted over encrypted channels (HTTPS)
  • Credentials and tokens are stored server-side and treated as confidential
  • Access is restricted to authorized systems and personnel

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with service providers needed to run the App, subject to confidentiality and security obligations.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data, and you may revoke integration access.

8. Revoke WHOOP Access

You can disconnect WHOOP integration in the App at any time. Upon disconnect, token-based access is revoked.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@yourdomain.com

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
